IUH

The forerunner of the Industrial University of Ho Chi Minh University was Professional Training School in Vo Vap and founded December 1956. March 1999, the school was upgraded to College Industry IV and December 2004 School was upgraded to Ho Chi Minh University of Industry under Decision No 214/2004/QD - TTg of the Prime Minister.
